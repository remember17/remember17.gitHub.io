Now Mac Subscription Terms

🗓 Subscription Period

  • 1 Month (Auto-Renewable Monthly)
  • 12 Months (Auto-Renewable Yearly)

💳 Subscription Price

Subject to the information submitted for In-App Purchase (IAP), for example, CNY 6 per month or CNY 58 per year.

🎟 Payment

Upon confirmation of purchase and payment, the charge will be applied to your iTunes (Apple ID) account.

🎫 Renewal

Your Apple iTunes account will be charged within 24 hours before the current subscription period expires. Upon successful payment, the subscription will be extended for another subscription period.

🤝 Cancel Subscription

To cancel renewal, please manually turn off the auto-renewal feature in your iTunes/Apple ID settings at least 24 hours before the current subscription period expires.
